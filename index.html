I'm a Fullstack Web Developer to be

Hi there!! Welcome to my Portfolio Website. I have put this page together to show you a little bit of my newest challenge. Changing career paths. I have worked pretty much my entire adult life in the Hospitality Business: bars, hotels, restaurants, and clubs. It is fun! But like everything in life comes to an end, I have decided to try and become a developer. I have taken on the challenge of studying at the Code Institute Software Developer Program. It is a completely different type of job, as you can imagine, and considering everything that has been going on around the world the IT branch has become extremely attractive. The demand for developers is huge, and I am thrilled to be taking this new step in my life.
I hope this page can entertain you for a few minutes.
